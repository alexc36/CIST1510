Our Story

Dreams Unleashed, Memories Created!

A few years ago, I was planning on my son's 5th birthday party. I created a checklist full of necessities and started shopping. I must have stopped at 10 different stores, and still did not complete my checklist. I asked myself "If parties are so much fun, why is the planning so difficult?

That is the exact moment Dream Events was born. We believe that the planning should be as easy as enjoying the party!
